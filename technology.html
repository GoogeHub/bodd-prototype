One device. One platform. A complete picture of the human body.

From the hardware on the floor to the intelligence in the cloud — Bodd has built every layer of the pipeline. Here's how it works.

From scan to insight.

Most body scanning solutions stop at the hardware. They give you a device — and leave you to figure out the rest. How do you extract the 3D model? How do you derive meaningful measurements from raw scan data? How do you store it securely, integrate it with your systems, and make it useful over time?

Bodd has built the answer to every one of those questions — in a single, end-to-end platform. From the moment a person steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation receives actionable data, everything happens inside Bodd. Nothing is left for you to build, integrate, or manage.

The hardware

The hardware that makes everything possible.

Bodd's scanner is proudly Australian designed and precision manufactured by Bosch — one of the world's most trusted engineering organisations. The result is a device that performs to enterprise standards in any environment, from a military recruit training facility to a pharmacy floor.

The Bodd scanner is a self-contained, self-serve scanning unit engineered for enterprise deployment. It was designed from first principles to work in real-world environments — not controlled laboratory conditions — and to be operated by anyone, without training, in any environment.

The pipeline

Every layer of the pipeline, already built.

A scan is raw data. What makes it valuable is everything that happens next. Bodd has built the complete pipeline that transforms a rapid scan into structured, actionable intelligence — automatically, securely, and in real time.

Step 1

Scan

Advanced sensing captures thousands of data points across the body surface. Data is processed on-device immediately upon scan completion — no operator required, no specialist setting needed.

Step 2 — Bodd Core + modules

Process

Raw scan data is transformed into a high-fidelity 3D body model, up to 400 precise measurements, and — depending on activated modules — body composition analysis and key wellness indicators. Everything derived automatically, with no manual input.

Step 3 — BoddHub · MyBodd · integrations

Deliver

Results are surfaced in real time — to BoddHub for your organisation, to MyBodd for the individual where enabled, and via secure data feeds to any downstream systems you choose.

Step 4 — Over time

Grow

Every scan adds a new data point to the organisational and individual record. The more you scan, the richer the picture — enabling trend analysis, cohort comparisons, and population-level intelligence that delivers compounding value over time.

The module suite

Start with what you need. Activate more as you grow.

Every Bodd deployment begins with the scanner. Bodd Core comes standard and is always on. Every other module — Bodd SizeMatch, Bodd Compose, Bodd Vitals, Bodd Motion, and Bodd Insight — is activated on top based on your organisation's needs. The scanner supports them all, with no additional hardware required.

Infrastructure & security

Built for the organisations that can't afford to get this wrong.

Bodd is trusted with body data by defence forces, global airlines, and pharmaceutical companies. The infrastructure and security standards we apply reflect that responsibility.

Amazon Web Services — Select Technology Partner AWS Partner Network

Bodd is an AWS Select Technology Partner — a formally recognised tier within the AWS Partner Network, validated against AWS standards for technical capability and customer success. This isn't just a hosting choice. It's the foundation that makes our regional sovereignty commitments verifiable: dedicated AWS infrastructure in every major geography we operate in, so your data never leaves its jurisdiction.

Regional instances

Dedicated AWS infrastructure per region — not shared, not multi-tenant. Your data is stored where you operate and never moves without your explicit authorisation.

Secure data separation

Every organisation's data is stored in a dedicated, isolated environment. Your data remains entirely separate from any other organisation's.

Data sovereignty as standard

Your data is stored in your region, in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements. Bodd maintains dedicated regional instances across all major operating geographies.

Private by design

No personally identifiable information is ever captured or stored by Bodd during the scan process. Privacy is not a feature — it's a structural property of how the platform was built.

Encrypted in transit and at rest

All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols at every point in the pipeline.

Audit logging

All data access and system activity is logged and auditable — meeting the requirements of the most security-conscious organisations in the world.
