From device to insight — the complete pipeline.

Most solutions give you a device. Bodd gives you everything that follows — the 3D model, the measurement engine, the analytics layer, the enterprise dashboard, and a data asset that delivers compounding value over time. Every layer seamlessly connected out of the box, with simple, secure integration into the systems your organisation already uses. The result is actionable intelligence from day one — with no gaps to fill, no third-party dependencies to manage, and no pipeline left for your team to build.

The hardware that makes everything possible.

Every Bodd deployment begins with the scanner — proudly Australian designed and precision manufactured by Bosch. Self-serve, self-contained, and deployable in any environment, from a military recruit training facility to a pharmacy floor. No operator required. No specialist setting. Just step on.

The scanner is the physical foundation of the entire platform. Without it, nothing else exists. Bodd Core comes standard with every scanner and is always on — everything else is activated on top.

Start with what you need. Activate more as you grow.

Every Bodd deployment begins with Bodd Core — the foundation everything else builds on. Additional modules are activated based on your needs and can be added at any time.

Bodd CoreFoundational — every deployment
A single scan generates a high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements from head to toe. The data layer every other module builds on. Always included.
Bodd SizeMatchOptional module
Rules-based sizing intelligence for uniforms and high-function apparel. Every individual matched to the right size, automatically, at any scale.
Bodd ComposeOptional module
Complete body composition analysis. Fat content, muscle mass, visceral fat, water content, and bone density — non-invasively, in the same scan.
Bodd VitalsOptional module
Standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — non-invasive, no specialist setting required. Tracked over time.
Bodd MotionComing soon
Range of motion and movement analysis. Understand how your people move — and identify risks before they become injuries.
Bodd InsightComing soon
Analytics and intelligence at every scale — from organisation-level trends and cohort analysis to population-level insights that inform decisions well beyond a single deployment.

A platform for your organisation. And your people.

BoddHub — The Enterprise Dashboard

The organisation-facing platform where all scan data is managed, analysed, and acted on.

Scanner management
Monitor status, usage, and connectivity across all deployed units
Configuration
Configure sizing rules, module settings, and data outputs
Data & reporting
Population-level data, trend analysis, and exportable reports
Secure enterprise integration
Makes scan data available to your existing procurement, workforce, and health platforms
Compounding intelligence
Population-level insights that only become visible through data that builds at scale over time

MyBodd — The Individual's View

The individual-facing platform — giving scan subjects direct access to their own data at my.bodd.com. No app required.

Interactive 3D body model
Precise measurements, body composition, and wellness indicators
Record over time
Every scan adds to a personal history showing how their body has changed over time
Privacy architecture
Structurally separate from the organisation's data — never combined unless the individual creates an account
Optional by design
Activated at the organisation's discretion — sensitive environments can choose not to enable individual portal access

The more you scan, the smarter Bodd gets.

A single scan delivers immediate value. But the real power of Bodd emerges over time. Every scan adds a data point to a population record that grows richer with every visit — building a picture of your people that no other tool can provide. The organisations that deploy Bodd don't just get better sizing or better wellness data. They get a compounding intelligence asset.
