Supporting GLP-1 weight management programmes.

As GLP-1 weight management medications become increasingly common, the need for objective body data that builds over time has never been greater. Bodd gives weight management centres and the individuals they support a rigorous baseline and a consistent tracking mechanism — capturing body composition changes over time, beyond weight alone.

Body weight tells one part of the story. Body composition — the ratio of muscle mass to fat, visceral fat levels, and water content — tells the rest. Bodd captures all of it, in a single non-invasive scan, as often as your programme requires.

Beyond the scale

GLP-1 medications affect muscle mass as well as fat. Bodd captures the full picture — so clients and clinicians can see exactly what's changing, and what isn't.

Monthly progress tracking

Bring clients back every month for a rapid, non-invasive scan. Each visit adds a data point — building an objective record of progress that motivates clients and demonstrates programme outcomes.

Rigorous baseline

Establish a comprehensive baseline before a programme begins — body composition, key measurements, and wellness indicators — so every subsequent scan is measured against a clear starting point.

Demonstrate outcomes

Objective before-and-after data for every client. The evidence base to demonstrate programme efficacy — to clients, to referring clinicians, and to insurers.

Give every client their own MyBodd.

When your centre enables MyBodd, every scan becomes something the client owns. Through MyBodd — Bodd's optional consumer portal — clients can access their body composition results, progress record, and wellness indicators the moment their scan is complete. Seeing the data themselves is one of the most powerful drivers of engagement and retention.

Body composition results

Fat content, muscle mass, visceral fat, and water content — tracked over time, explained clearly, always in context of where they started.

Progress that's visible to them

Every scan adds a data point to their personal record. When clients can see exactly what's changing — and what isn't — they stay motivated and stay coming back.

Wellness indicators

Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate — presented as personal wellness tracking data to complement their progress picture.

Data sharing — always their choice

Clients decide who else can see their data — referring practitioners, insurers, or no one. Always opt-in, always revocable.

How it works

From step-on to insight — the complete pipeline.

Every Bodd deployment follows the same five-step process — from the moment an individual steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation has actionable intelligence.

1
Step on

The individual steps onto the scanner and follows simple audio-visual prompts. No operator. No specialist setting. No need to change clothing. The experience takes under a minute and requires nothing from your team.

2
Scan completes

A single, non-invasive pass captures thousands of data points across the complete human body — head to toe, in one continuous process. No contact. No discomfort. No privacy concerns.

3
Model & measurements generated

A high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements are generated automatically — the data foundation every other module builds on. Available in BoddHub instantly.

4
Modules deliver

Depending on your deployment: precise size matches via Bodd SizeMatch. Body composition results via Bodd Compose. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate via Bodd Vitals. Every result available the moment the scan completes — no waiting, no processing lag.

5
Intelligence compounds

Every scan adds to a population dataset that grows richer over time — surfacing trends, patterns, and insights that no point-in-time measurement can provide. This layer of intelligence is unlocked via Bodd Insight.

What to tell your members.

When members ask about the scan, your team can answer with confidence. Here's what happens — and what doesn't.

No personal information captured

The scanner captures body shape data — not names, faces, or any personally identifiable information. Members scan against a unique code that only your organisation can connect to an individual.

Their data stays with you

Scan results are stored securely in your BoddHub instance — in your region, under your control. Members can optionally create a MyBodd account to access their own data directly, but this is always their choice.

Non-invasive and comfortable

The scan requires no physical contact, no removal of clothing, and no uncomfortable equipment. Members stand on the scanner platform and follow simple on-screen prompts — that's it.

Works with the systems you already use.

Bodd doesn't ask you to replace your existing infrastructure — it integrates with it. BoddHub makes your scan data available to your existing systems — so it flows directly into your procurement, HR, or health workflows without manual export or re-entry.

Secure data transfer

Your scan data flows securely from BoddHub into your existing systems — whether that's a procurement platform, HR system, or health record solution. Your data, delivered where you need it, without manual export or re-entry.

Data sovereignty

Your data stays in your region. Bodd operates dedicated AWS instances per client — your data is never co-mingled with another organisation's, and always stored in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements.

Exportable data

All scan data is exportable in standard formats — ready for supplier portals, procurement systems, clinical dashboards, or any other downstream tool your organisation relies on.
