Turn your pharmacy into an in-store health and wellness assessment hub.

Your customers already trust you with their health. Bodd gives you the tools to act on that trust — delivering a fast, non-invasive body assessment that no other retail environment can offer. No specialist staff required. No separate booking system. No clinical setting needed. Just a scanner, a customer, and 60 seconds.

From body composition and key measurements to standing blood pressure, heart rate, and breathing rate — Bodd captures a complete physical picture at the point of sale. In your store. As part of a normal visit.

Bodd Vitals — wellness indicators

Standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — captured non-invasively, no cuff required, in the same scan.

Bodd Compose — body composition

Fat content, muscle mass, visceral fat, water content, and bone density — the data that transforms a wellness conversation from general to genuinely personal.

BoddHub — in your hands

Every scan result is available instantly in BoddHub — your pharmacist's dashboard for customer data, progress tracking, and programme management.

Body data that powers better product recommendations.

When you know what's actually going on inside someone's body, the conversation changes completely. Bodd gives your team the objective data to recommend the right products — not based on what a customer thinks they need, but on what their body data shows.

From weight management nutrition and protein supplementation to compression garments, skincare, vitamins and minerals, and beyond — every recommendation becomes evidence-based. Every product conversation becomes a personalised consultation.

Nutrition & supplementation

Low muscle mass, high visceral fat, vitamin deficiency indicators — body composition data gives your team a clinical basis for nutrition and supplement recommendations.

Compression & fit-based products

Precise body measurements enable accurate sizing recommendations for compression garments, support wear, and any product where fit determines efficacy.

Skincare & personal wellness

Body composition and wellness indicator data opens meaningful conversations about skincare, hydration, and personal wellness products — grounded in what the data actually shows.

Weight management programmes

Match customers to the right programme — and the right products within it — based on their actual body composition profile, not self-reported weight alone.

Every scan is a reason to come back.

The most valuable customer relationship is one that compounds over time. Bodd creates a natural, structured reason for customers to return to your pharmacy every month — not just to collect a prescription, but to check in on their progress, see what's changed, and continue a conversation that's genuinely meaningful to them.

And because every scan adds a new data point to their record, each visit is more valuable than the last. The data gets richer. The recommendations get sharper. The relationship gets deeper.

Structured repeat visit cadence

Monthly progress scans tied to programme milestones give customers a concrete reason to return — and give your team a structured touchpoint to reinforce the relationship.

CRM & loyalty programme integration

Bodd makes scan data available to your existing CRM and loyalty platforms — so it feeds directly into your customer engagement and rewards workflows.

Loyalty through outcomes

Customers who can see their progress stay. Customers who stay refer others. The data Bodd generates doesn't just drive repeat visits — it drives the kind of loyalty that no points programme can replicate.

Weight management support without ongoing progress tracking is a missed opportunity.

An individual who starts a weight management programme without a precise body composition baseline has no objective way to measure their progress. A pharmacy that can't track member outcomes has no way to demonstrate the value of the programme — or to keep individuals engaged and returning month after month. Without the right data, the conversation between pharmacist and member is guesswork. With it, it becomes a meaningful, evidence-based wellness partnership.

How it works

From step-on to insight — the complete pipeline.

Every Bodd deployment follows the same five-step process — from the moment an individual steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation has actionable intelligence.

1
Step on

The individual steps onto the scanner and follows simple audio-visual prompts. No operator. No specialist setting. No need to change clothing. The experience takes under a minute and requires nothing from your team.

2
Scan completes

A single, non-invasive pass captures thousands of data points across the complete human body — head to toe, in one continuous process. No contact. No discomfort. No privacy concerns.

3
Model & measurements generated

A high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements are generated automatically — the data foundation every other module builds on. Available in BoddHub instantly.

4
Modules deliver

Depending on your deployment: precise size matches via Bodd SizeMatch. Body composition results via Bodd Compose. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate via Bodd Vitals. Every result available the moment the scan completes — no waiting, no processing lag.

5
Intelligence compounds

Every scan adds to a population dataset that grows richer over time — surfacing trends, patterns, and insights that no point-in-time measurement can provide. This layer of intelligence is unlocked via Bodd Insight.

What to tell your members.

When members ask about the scan, your team can answer with confidence. Here's what happens — and what doesn't.

No personal information captured

The scanner captures body shape data — not names, faces, or any personally identifiable information. Members scan against a unique code that only your organisation can connect to an individual.

Their data stays with you

Scan results are stored securely in your BoddHub instance — in your region, under your control. Members can optionally create a MyBodd account to access their own data directly, but this is always their choice.

Non-invasive and comfortable

The scan requires no physical contact, no removal of clothing, and no uncomfortable equipment. Members stand on the scanner platform and follow simple on-screen prompts — that's it.
