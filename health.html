The baseline everything else is built on.

In health and wellness, the most important scan is the first one. It establishes the baseline — the complete picture of where an individual is today — against which every subsequent programme or goal is measured.

Until now, establishing that baseline has been slow, fragmented, and expensive. Different devices for different measurements. Specialist operators. Multiple appointments. Bodd captures everything in a single, non-invasive scan. Bodd Core delivers a high-fidelity 3D body model and precise measurements head to toe — the foundation every other module builds on. Add Bodd Compose for body composition, Bodd Vitals for key wellness indicators, and Bodd Motion for movement analysis. In your space. With no specialist training required.

Give every individual their own MyBodd.

When an organisation enables MyBodd, the value of a scan extends far beyond your dashboard. Through MyBodd — Bodd's optional consumer portal — every individual gets their own personal health and wellness record the moment their scan is complete. MyBodd is always optional and always controlled by your organisation. When activated, it deepens individual engagement, supports behaviour change, and turns a one-time assessment into an ongoing personal wellness journey — creating a reason to return that no appointment reminder can replicate.

How it works

From step-on to insight — the complete pipeline.

Every Bodd deployment follows the same five-step process — from the moment an individual steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation has actionable intelligence.

1
Step on

The individual steps onto the scanner and follows simple audio-visual prompts. No operator. No specialist setting. No need to change clothing. The experience takes under a minute and requires nothing from your team.

2
Scan completes

A single, non-invasive pass captures thousands of data points across the complete human body — head to toe, in one continuous process. No contact. No discomfort. No privacy concerns.

3
Model & measurements generated

A high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements are generated automatically — the data foundation every other module builds on. Available in BoddHub instantly.

4
Modules deliver

Depending on your deployment: precise size matches via Bodd SizeMatch. Body composition results via Bodd Compose. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate via Bodd Vitals. Every result available the moment the scan completes.

5
Intelligence compounds

Every scan adds to a population dataset that grows richer over time — surfacing trends, patterns, and insights that no point-in-time measurement can provide. Unlocked via Bodd Insight.

What to tell your members.

When members ask about the scan, your team can answer with confidence. Here's what happens — and what doesn't.

No personal information captured

The scanner captures body shape data only — not names, faces, or any personally identifiable information. Members scan against a unique code that only your organisation can connect to an individual.

Their data stays with you

Results are stored securely in your BoddHub instance — in your region, under your control. Members can optionally create a MyBodd account to access their own data, but this is always their choice.

Non-invasive and comfortable

No physical contact, no removal of clothing, no uncomfortable equipment. Members stand on the scanner platform and follow simple on-screen prompts — nothing more.

Up and running faster than you’d expect.

A Bodd deployment doesn’t require months of IT project management or specialist infrastructure. Here’s how it works from order to first scan.

1
Hardware deployed

Scanners shipped, positioned, and connected — WiFi, LAN, or SIM. No specialist installation required.

2
Platform configured

BoddHub set up with your module configuration and data outputs. Connected to your existing systems where required.

3
Team briefed

Onboarding covers BoddHub, reporting, and day-to-day management. Because the scanner is self-serve, no specialist training is required.

4
First scan, day one

Data flows from the first scan. No ramp-up period. Every result in BoddHub immediately — ready from day one.

Works with the systems you already use.

Bodd doesn't ask you to replace your existing infrastructure — it integrates with it. BoddHub makes your scan data available to your existing systems — so it flows directly into your procurement, HR, or health workflows without manual export or re-entry.

Secure data transfer

Your scan data flows securely from BoddHub into your existing systems — whether that's a procurement platform, HR system, or health record solution. Your data, delivered where you need it, without manual export or re-entry.

Data sovereignty

Your data stays in your region. Bodd operates dedicated AWS instances per client — your data is never co-mingled with another organisation's, and always stored in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements.

Exportable data

All scan data is exportable in standard formats — ready for supplier portals, procurement systems, clinical dashboards, or any other downstream tool your organisation relies on.
