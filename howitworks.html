The Bodd ecosystem

Not just a scanner. Not just software.

Bodd is a connected system — hardware, software, and an intelligence layer — designed to work as a whole. Most organisations encounter it through a single, concrete problem: sizing a uniformed workforce, establishing a health baseline for recruits, or launching a body composition programme. Bodd solves that problem well. But the underlying system is built for more.

At one end is the Bodd Body Scanner — a clinical-grade, self-serve device that captures precise 3D body data in under 30 seconds. That data flows into BoddHub, your organisation's secure enterprise dashboard, where intelligence modules translate measurements into decisions. The value isn't in any single component. It's in how they connect.

The flow

From scan to decision in under a minute.

The path from a body scan to an actionable output is fast, automatic, and consistent — regardless of who's operating it, where it's deployed, or how many people are moving through it.

01 — Scan
A person steps in front of the scanner.
In under 30 seconds, the Bodd Body Scanner captures up to 400 precise measurements — full body, feet, hands, and head. No trained operator. No changing room. No tape measure.
02 — Secure
Data routes to your instance.
Measurement data is sent immediately to your organisation's BoddHub instance — hosted in your region, on your infrastructure. No personally identifiable information is captured during the scan itself.
03 — Apply
The right intelligence activates.
The platform applies the intelligence module configured for your use case — SizeMatch for uniform procurement, Vitals for wellness indicators, Composition for body composition tracking. The output is always specific to your needs.
04 — Act
Your team has what it needs.
Sizing recommendations, wellness baselines, health trends, and operational dashboards — available immediately. Decisions that previously required weeks of manual work are now consistent and automatic.
