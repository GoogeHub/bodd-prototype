Home / About / Work With Us

We hire for values as much as skills.

Bodd is a Great Place to Work® certified organisation building something genuinely new in the world. If you want to do the best work of your career alongside people who care deeply about getting it right — read on.

What we're looking for.

We're a small team doing something ambitious. That means we need people who can operate with a high degree of autonomy, who take real ownership of their work, and who are energised — not intimidated — by problems that don't have obvious answers yet. Technical skills matter. Values matter more. You can learn a tool. You can't learn to give a damn.

Ownership

We take real pride and responsibility in growing this business. When something goes wrong, we own it. When something goes right, we share it. We don't do blame culture or credit-grabbing.

Creative problem solvers

Curiosity drives us. We ask questions before we assume answers, experiment constantly, and learn always. If you need a playbook for every decision, this probably isn't the place for you.

Good human beings

Tight-knit. Honest. Always respectful. No arrogance. We don't have time for jerks, even brilliant ones. If you treat people well regardless of their title, you'll fit right in.

Courageous & humble

Comfortable with the unknown. Optimism is essential. False bravado and arrogance are not tolerated. We want people who back themselves without losing sight of what they don't yet know.

High performing & trusted

A-grade team. Trust without micro-management. The organisations we work with demand nothing less — and so do we. We set a high bar and we hold each other to it, with respect.

Love work, love life

Proud of what we do. We prioritise happiness, physical and mental wellbeing. Great Place to Work® certified — not because we put it on the website, but because our people actually said so.

Don't see your role?

We're always interested in exceptional people — even when we don't have an active role that fits. If you think you belong here, send us a note. Tell us who you are, what you do, and why Bodd.

We read every application. We aim to respond within five business days.

Great Place to Work® certified

Not because we put it on the website. Because our people said so. Bodd is independently certified by Great Place to Work® — the global authority on workplace culture.
