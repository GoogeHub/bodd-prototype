Our values

Do what you say

We all have a role to play in nurturing this business — it's collectively ours. If you say it, do it. No exceptions.

Creative problem solvers

We think fast, move fast, and stay curious — innovation is how we operate. We work in regulated industries where the stakes are high and stay agile within it.

Good human beings

We genuinely care about the people around us. That means being straight talkers — clear, honest, and respectful. No dickheads, even brilliant ones.

Courageous & humble

We're laying the track as we race over it. Curious, comfortable, and excited by the unknown. Optimism is essential. False bravado is not tolerated.

High performing & trusted

We hire A-grade people and trust them to deliver. Energy is contagious — bring calm to the pace.

Love work, love life

We're proud of the work we do. We know when to push and when to step back. Burning out doesn't serve ourselves, our mission, or each other.
