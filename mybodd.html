Home / MyBodd

Your body. Your data. Your story.

Your Bodd scan captures something remarkable — a complete, precise picture of your body. Through MyBodd, that picture is yours to explore, understand, and watch change over time — privately and securely.

Access My Results at my.bodd.com →
[ Hero image — individual viewing their MyBodd results on a phone ]

Most people go through life never truly knowing their body — not in numbers, not in detail, not over time. A Bodd scan changes that. In a single rapid scan, it captures a level of body data that simply wasn't accessible before — and through MyBodd, all of it is yours.

What you'll see

A complete picture of you. Right now — and over time.

Your 3D body model

See yourself in three dimensions. Your Bodd scan generates a precise, interactive 3D replica of your body — accurate to the millimetre. Rotate it. Explore it. And if you're scanned again in the future, watch how it changes.

Your measurements

Every dimension, precisely captured — height, chest, waist, hips, inseam, and far more. No guesswork. No tape measure. The most accurate set of body measurements you've ever had.

Your body composition

If Bodd Compose is activated

What your body is actually made of — fat content, muscle mass, visceral fat, water content, and bone density estimation — presented clearly, with the context to help you understand what it means.

Your key health & wellness indicators

If Bodd Vitals is activated

Standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — captured during your scan, displayed as personal reference data and tracked over time.

Your record over time

If you've been scanned more than once

Every scan adds a new chapter to your story. Body composition. Measurements. Health and wellness indicators. Side by side, over time — so you can see exactly what's changing, and by how much.

Insights for every body.

One scan is a snapshot. Every scan that follows is a story.

Most health and wellness data gives you a point in time. MyBodd gives you a trajectory — a record of your body that grows more meaningful with every visit. Whether you're tracking progress on a wellness programme, monitoring how your body responds to training, or simply curious about how you're changing over time — MyBodd gives you the clearest possible picture.

How it works

Your results are ready the moment your scan is complete.

Step 1

Start your scan

Your scan begins with a transaction code or QR code provided at the kiosk. This is the only identifier Bodd needs — no name, no email, no personal details.

Step 2

Complete your scan

Follow the simple prompts on screen. When your scan is complete, a QR code appears on the kiosk display.

Step 3

See your results instantly

Scan the QR code with your phone and you're taken directly to your personal results page — your 3D body model, measurements, body composition, and health and wellness indicators, ready to explore. At this point, Bodd still knows nothing about you. Your results are completely anonymous.

Step 4

Create a MyBodd account Recommended

To save your results and access them anytime, create a free MyBodd account before you leave the scanner. Sign up with your email address or use Google, Facebook, or other supported providers. This is the moment you choose to build a direct relationship with Bodd — entirely on your terms.

Step 5

MyBodd, unlocked

With a MyBodd account, you can track your body over time, share your data with providers and brands, and manage everything from one place. You see your data before we know who you are. Creating a MyBodd account is always your choice — not a requirement to access your results.

A note on anonymous results

Your results are accessible via the QR code shown at the end of your scan. If you close that session without creating a MyBodd account, your results won't be accessible again — because at that point, Bodd has no way of identifying you. We recommend creating a MyBodd account before you leave the scanner. If you do lose access, the simplest solution is to scan again next time you're at a Bodd kiosk.

Share your data

Your data. Your rules.

MyBodd lets you decide who else gets to see your data — and put it to work for you across the services and experiences that matter most. Sharing is always opt-in, always controlled by you, and always revocable.

Shop smarter — ecommerce & apparel brands

Share your precise measurements with any brand that supports MyBodd — and get sizing recommendations, made-to-measure options, and personalised fit suggestions that are actually accurate. No more guessing between sizes. No more returns.

Arrive ready — allied health & GP clinics

Share your complete physical baseline with your GP, physiotherapist, osteopath, or allied health practitioner before your appointment. No starting from scratch. Just a complete picture of your body, already in their hands.

Train smarter — personal trainers & nutritionists

Give your trainer or nutritionist precise body composition data — muscle mass, fat content, visceral fat, and more — from day one. Better data means better programmes, clearer progress tracking, and results that are actually measurable.

Perform better — sporting clubs & coaches

Share body composition, measurements, and physical data with your club, coach, or sporting organisation. Whether it's kit fitting, performance programming, or physical readiness tracking — your data gives the people supporting you a better foundation to work from.

Unlock better cover — health insurance

Some health insurers reward members who can demonstrate their physical health and wellness. Share your body composition and health and wellness indicator data to support applications, wellness incentive programmes, or premium reviews.

Start stronger — new employers & workplaces

Starting a new job that requires a uniform? Share your sizing data directly with your new employer's Bodd deployment — so your kit is ordered correctly before your first day, without a fitting session.

Privacy & security

Private by design. Secure by default. Yours to control.

No PII ever captured

Bodd never captures or stores your name, date of birth, contact details, or employee ID during the scan process. Your scan exists in Bodd's system linked only to a unique code.

You choose whether to create an account

You can access your results using just your unique scan code — no account required. Creating a MyBodd account is always your choice.

Your identity stays separate

The pharmacy, gym, employer, or provider that arranged your scan holds the connection between your scan code and your identity. Bodd never has access to that link.

Your data stays in your region

Bodd maintains dedicated regional infrastructure in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements. Your data is stored in your region and never leaves your jurisdiction.

Your consent, always

Before any data is captured, you'll be asked to give consent on the scanner. Consent for research use is separate and always opt-in. Neither is assumed.

You're in control

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your scan data at any time. Contact privacy@bodd.com or the provider that arranged your scan.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Can my employer see my personal body data?

If you were scanned through your employer, they can see scan data linked to your unique scan code — and they hold the connection between that code and your identity within their own systems. For specifics, your HR or people team is the right first contact.

Will my data be shared with anyone else?

No. Bodd does not sell, share, or licence individual scan data to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide the Bodd platform services to the provider that arranged your scan.

What if I want my data deleted?

You can request deletion of your scan data by contacting the provider that arranged your scan, or by emailing privacy@bodd.com. We'll process your request in accordance with applicable privacy legislation.

I've been scanned at more than one place. Can I see all my results in one place?

Yes — that's exactly what MyBodd is built for. Create a personal account at my.bodd.com and your scans from any Bodd deployment come together in a single record that builds over time.

I've been scanned before. Can I see my previous results?

If your previous scan was captured through the same provider using the same scan code, your historical results will be visible in MyBodd alongside your most recent scan. If you can't find previous results, contact the provider that arranged your scans.

Is MyBodd available everywhere?

Access to MyBodd depends on how your Bodd deployment has been configured. Some settings — including certain defence, government, and security environments — may not enable individual access. If you're unsure, contact the organisation that arranged your scan.

Still have questions?

If you have questions about your scan, your data, or how to access your results, we're here to help.
