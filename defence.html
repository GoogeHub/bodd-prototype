Ill-fitting kit isn't just uncomfortable. In the field, it's a liability.

In defence, fit has operational consequences. A ballistic vest with coverage gaps leaves a soldier exposed in exactly the situation it was designed to protect against. Boots that don't fit cause injuries that take personnel off the line. Load-bearing equipment that sits wrong degrades performance across a sustained operation.

Traditional sizing methods — manual measurement, self-reporting, tape measures — are slow, inconsistent, and don't scale. They leave procurement teams making decisions on data they can't fully trust. The result: remakes, emergency replacements, and personnel waiting for kit they need now.

Size an entire recruit intake in hours, not months.

Bodd replaces the manual sizing process with a rapid, non-invasive self-serve scan. No trained operator. No manual measurement. No data entry errors. The New Zealand Defence Force uses Bodd to scan 100 recruits in 100 minutes — with throughput that scales linearly at any volume. Every measurement your procurement team needs — for every item of kit, from boots to ballistic protection — captured in a single pass.

Bodd is the only platform globally with dedicated high-fidelity scanning for the complete human body — full body, feet, hands, and head.

The scanner your uniform team deploys is also your most powerful wellness assessment tool.

Bodd Compose and Bodd Vitals are optional modules — activated in addition to your core sizing deployment, at your discretion. Once switched on, they require no additional hardware and no additional scanning time. The same scan that sizes your people for kit now also captures complete body composition and key wellness indicators — standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — tracked over time across your force.

For human performance teams managing the physical readiness of large populations, this is transformative — enabling proactive wellbeing support, tracking physical trends, and building a population-level picture of workforce health that no other tool can provide.

How it works

From step-on to insight — the complete pipeline.

Every Bodd deployment follows the same five-step process — from the moment an individual steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation has actionable intelligence.

1
Step on

The individual steps onto the scanner and follows simple audio-visual prompts. No operator. No specialist setting. No need to change clothing. The experience takes under a minute and requires nothing from your team.

2
Scan completes

A single, non-invasive pass captures thousands of data points across the complete human body — head to toe, in one continuous process. No contact. No discomfort. No privacy concerns.

3
Model & measurements generated

A high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements are generated automatically — the data foundation every other module builds on. Available in BoddHub instantly.

4
Modules deliver

Depending on your deployment: precise size matches via Bodd SizeMatch. Body composition results via Bodd Compose. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate via Bodd Vitals. Every result available the moment the scan completes — no waiting, no processing lag.

5
Intelligence compounds

Every scan adds to a population dataset that grows richer over time — surfacing trends, patterns, and insights that no point-in-time measurement can provide. This layer of intelligence is unlocked via Bodd Insight.

Up and running faster than you'd expect.

A Bodd deployment doesn't require months of IT project management or specialist infrastructure. Here's how it works from order to first scan.

1
Hardware deployed

Scanners are shipped, positioned, and connected — WiFi, LAN, or SIM. No specialist installation required. Your team doesn't need to be involved.

2
Platform configured

BoddHub is set up with your sizing rules, module configuration, and data outputs. Connected to your existing procurement, workforce, or health systems where required.

3
Your team briefed

Because the scanner is self-serve, your team doesn't need specialist training. A brief onboarding session covers BoddHub, reporting, and how to manage the scanner day-to-day.

4
First scan, day one

Data flows from the first scan. No ramp-up period. No data wrangling. Every result available in BoddHub immediately — ready for procurement, clinical review, or reporting from day one.

Works with the systems you already use.

Bodd doesn't ask you to replace your existing infrastructure — it integrates with it. BoddHub makes your scan data available to your existing systems — so it flows directly into your procurement, HR, or health workflows without manual export or re-entry.

Secure data transfer

Your scan data flows securely from BoddHub into your existing systems — whether that's a procurement platform, HR system, or health record solution. Your data, delivered where you need it, without manual export or re-entry.

Data sovereignty

Your data stays in your region. Bodd operates dedicated AWS instances per client — your data is never co-mingled with another organisation's, and always stored in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements.

Exportable data

All scan data is exportable in standard formats — ready for supplier portals, procurement systems, clinical dashboards, or any other downstream tool your organisation relies on.
