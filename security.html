Body data is among the most sensitive data an organisation can hold. We built Bodd accordingly.

From the architecture of the scanner to the infrastructure that stores your data — privacy and security aren't features we added. They're properties of how Bodd was built.

Privacy architecture — built in from day one.

Most platforms are built first, then secured. Bodd was designed with privacy as a structural property — not a layer applied afterwards.

No PII captured during scanning

The scanner never captures personally identifiable information. Every individual scans against a unique transaction code — a reference number that holds no identity data of its own.

The organisation holds the key

The organisation deploying Bodd holds the mapping between transaction codes and individual identities. Bodd holds the scan data. The two exist in separate systems — never combined within Bodd's infrastructure.

Individual access is optional by design

Individuals can choose to create a MyBodd account to access their own data directly — but this is their choice, not a default. Organisations in sensitive environments can disable individual portal access entirely.

Data sovereignty as standard.

Your data stays in your region. Bodd operates dedicated regional infrastructure — not a shared global environment — so your organisation's data is always stored in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements.

Regional AWS instances

Dedicated cloud infrastructure per region. Your data never moves outside your designated geography without your explicit authorisation.

Secure data separation

Bodd operates regional cloud instances, with every organisation's data stored in a dedicated, isolated environment within that infrastructure. Your data remains entirely separate from any other organisation's.

Four principles. Every deployment. Every market.

Transparency

Individuals are always informed when a scan is taking place and what data is being captured.

Consent

Scanning is always opt-in. No individual is scanned without their knowledge and agreement.

Security

Enterprise-grade infrastructure, encryption, and access controls — applied consistently across every deployment.

Compliance

Bodd meets the regulatory requirements of every market it operates in — and is actively pursuing additional certifications.

Infrastructure.

Amazon Web Services — Select Technology Partner AWS Partner Network

Bodd is an AWS Select Technology Partner — a formally recognised tier within the AWS Partner Network, validated against AWS standards for technical capability and customer success. Our regional sovereignty commitments are backed by dedicated AWS infrastructure in every major geography we operate in. Your data never leaves its designated jurisdiction.

Encryption in transit and at rest
All data encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.2+ in transit.
Role-based access controls
Strict controls govern who within your organisation can access what data, and when.
Audit logging
All data access and system activity is logged and available for review.
Regional instances
Dedicated infrastructure per region — no data co-mingling across client instances.

A note on Bodd Vitals

Bodd Vitals captures key wellness indicators — standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — non-invasively during the scan process. These are presented as wellness tracking data points, not clinically validated medical readings. Bodd Vitals is currently pursuing TGA, FDA, and CE Mark registration. Current deployments operate as precision wellness tracking tools.
