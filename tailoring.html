Manual measurement is the weakest link in tailoring.

Made-to-measure and tailored clothing depends entirely on accurate body measurements. Yet the traditional process is inconsistent by design. Measurements vary between fitters. Clients shift, flex, or hold tension without realising. A centimetre off on the chest or a mis-read inseam can mean the difference between a garment that fits beautifully and one that goes back for alteration.

Beyond accuracy, there's the time cost. A full manual measurement session takes 15 to 20 minutes per client — time that limits throughput, adds friction to the buying experience, and scales poorly as your business grows. For made-to-measure operations running volume, or retailers looking to add a tailoring service, the manual process is the bottleneck. Bodd removes it entirely.

The 3D advantage.

Every Bodd scan generates a high-fidelity 3D body model — a one-to-one digital replica of the customer's body. Beyond sizing, this opens possibilities for virtual fitting, product development, and grading decisions.

Give every customer their own MyBodd.

When you enable MyBodd, the value of a Bodd scan extends far beyond your fitting room. Through MyBodd — Bodd's optional consumer portal — every customer gets their own personal measurement record the moment their scan is complete. Their 3D body model, precise measurements, and sizing profile — accessible anywhere, at any time, on any device.

Their measurements, always with them

Customers can access their full measurement profile from any device — so when they return, their sizing is already on file. No re-measuring. No starting from scratch.

Shop smarter across any brand

Customers can share their MyBodd profile with any brand that supports Bodd — getting precise sizing recommendations wherever they shop. Every scan you take becomes part of a lasting relationship with their wardrobe.

Private by design

MyBodd is always opt-in. Customers choose whether to create an account, what to store, and who to share it with. No PII is captured during the scan — identity and measurements are structurally separate until a customer chooses to link them.

How it works

From step-on to insight — the complete pipeline.

Every Bodd deployment follows the same five-step process — from the moment an individual steps onto the scanner to the moment your organisation has actionable intelligence.

1
Step on

The individual steps onto the scanner and follows simple audio-visual prompts. No operator. No specialist setting. No need to change clothing. The experience takes under a minute and requires nothing from your team.

2
Scan completes

A single, non-invasive pass captures thousands of data points across the complete human body — head to toe, in one continuous process. No contact. No discomfort. No privacy concerns.

3
Model & measurements generated

A high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements are generated automatically — the data foundation every other module builds on. Available in BoddHub instantly.

4
Modules deliver

Depending on your deployment: precise size matches via Bodd SizeMatch. Body composition results via Bodd Compose. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate via Bodd Vitals. Every result available the moment the scan completes — no waiting, no processing lag.

5
Intelligence compounds

Every scan adds to a population dataset that grows richer over time — surfacing trends, patterns, and insights that no point-in-time measurement can provide. This layer of intelligence is unlocked via Bodd Insight.
