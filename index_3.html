Intelligence modules

Bodd Core

The foundation of every Bodd deployment. A single scan captures a high-fidelity 3D body model and up to 400 precise measurements from head to toe.

Bodd SizeMatch

Rapid, accurate sizing for uniforms and high-function apparel. Bodd's rules-based sizing engine matches every person to the right size — consistently, accurately, at any scale.

Bodd Compose

Complete body composition analysis. Fat content, muscle mass, visceral fat, water content, and bone density — captured quickly, accurately, and non-invasively.

Bodd Vitals

Key wellness indicators, non-invasively captured. Standing blood pressure, heart rate, heart rate variability, and breathing rate — tracked over time, no specialist setting required.

Bodd Motion Coming soon

Range of motion and movement analysis. Understand how your people move — and identify risks before they become injuries.

Bodd Insight Coming soon

Analytics and intelligence at every scale — from organisation-level trends and cohort analysis to population-level insights that inform decisions well beyond a single deployment.

Trust & privacy

Built on a foundation of trust.

Body data is among the most sensitive data an organisation can hold. Bodd was designed from the ground up with that responsibility in mind.

Private by design

No PII is captured during the scan process. Every subject scans against a unique transaction code — the organisation holds the key that connects data to individuals.

Location controlled data storage

Your data is stored in your region, in accordance with local data sovereignty requirements.

Responsible custodianship

Bodd operates by four guiding principles: Transparency, Consent, Security, and Compliance — across every deployment, every market, every customer.

Our story

Built by founders who lived the problem.

Bodd was founded in Melbourne by childhood friends Rob Fisher and David McLaughlin — two entrepreneurs who couldn't find a sizing solution good enough for their own apparel business, so they built their own.

What started as a prototype to solve the challenge of accurate body measurement has become a globally deployed body data platform trusted by defence forces, global airlines, and pharmaceutical companies worldwide.

"Body data is incredibly powerful, but it's also incredibly sensitive. Getting the balance right between innovation, regulation, and trust is critical."

Rob Fisher, Co-founder & CEO
